Our Solution

AMBER alerts and similar alert systems aren't effective. With inefficient utilization and success rates, it's evident that we need a better solution. Vigilant provides an integrated framework for reporting child abductions with artificial intelligence techniques to streamline the process. Here are some of our features:

  • Report system for organizing and reporting abductions
  • Implementation and integration of Virginia surveillance cameras onto the website
  • Five uses of machine learning, including license plate detection, facial recognition, and vehicular recognition
  • A comprehensive log-in and administrative system for governmental officials and registered contributors to input alerts
